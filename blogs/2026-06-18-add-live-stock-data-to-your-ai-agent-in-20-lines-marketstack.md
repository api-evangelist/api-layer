A tutorial on integrating real-time stock price data into an AI agent using Claude's tool-use capability, since language models can't recall accurate current prices from memory but can call external APIs. It walks through defining a stock price tool schema, fetching live prices from the Marketstack API's end-of-day endpoint, and building a complete agent in roughly 20 lines of Python. The Marketstack free tier provides 100 monthly requests, enough for prototyping agents tracking multiple tickers and historical data.
